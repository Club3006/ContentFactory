### 2. The "Skill" Ecosystem (Scrapers as Creators)
**Captured**: 2025-12-27
**Context**: Moving beyond simple transcription to intelligent generation.
**The Concept**:
- The app is a collection of "Highly Trained Skills" (Scrapers/Creators).
- **Core Skill**: Podcast Scraper (Existing).
- **Evolution**: 
    1. **Auto-Generate**: Automatically produce LinkedIn, Instagram, YouTube, and X content from a single transcript source.
    2. **Trend Intelligence**: Develop scrapers that understand *what is trending* in our specific niche (Private Lending, Single-Family, Multifamily, DSCR, Ground-up Construction) on YouTube/Instagram/LinkedIn.
- **Why**: To ensure our content is not just existent, but *relevant* and *viral* within our specific market.
